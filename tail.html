A dog’s tail can be used to do everything from communicate feelings to aiding in rapid movement changes and body shifts. But the tail does more than that. Indeed, one of its primary, and perhaps most important functions, is to provide the dog balance and stability in an otherwise unstable environment. You may be wondering how a dog’s tail is relevant to our $TAIL. Well, let me tell you.

To put it in the simplest of terms, $TAIL is the predominant Rewards token in the Sanin Inu ecosystem. Much like a dog is given a sense of balance and stability by its own tail, our $TAIL provides the backbone for our dream of a decentralized system. These tokens play a pivotal role in maintaining the balance of community and ecosystem alike, and is therefore crucial to our goals of decentralization.
